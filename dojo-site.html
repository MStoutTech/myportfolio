About us

Our Dojo Story

After years of training under different masters, we have developed our own unique approach to teaching that emphasizes both traditional techniques and modern self-defense applications. Our top priority is to create a welcoming space where students of all backgrounds 🌈✊🍉♀️++ can develop confidence, discipline, and respect without the intimidation and dismissive attitude often felt at other schools. We hope to pass on not just martial arts skills, but life lessons about perseverance and inner strength that have shaped us to be who we are today.
